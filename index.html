Postnerd is to help you get past nerds and get work done without spending a fortune. Utilizing insights from unconventional doers who do not settle for the "9 to 5 grind", our mission is to offer effective solutions to business problems with minimum fuss and maximum payout.

We do not need to become drones to defeat the drones—Postnerd is here to help you extract maximum wealth from free or low-cost tech solutions offered to the public while building up true human-to-human networks (not to be confused with social media profiles!).

Taking technology back

The year 2020 shook many people into realizing that the "normal" of yesteryear is not stable and that we should expect big changes across all sectors of society in terms of how we organize living spaces, labor, and resources. Here at Postnerd we are motivated to not passively wait as the future "happens" to us. Specifically, we are working on solutions to offer alternatives to things like:

  • automation when unneeded—e.g. phone robots when people do better
  • artificial intelligence—the complex fabric of life is not a mechanical chessboard!
  • overseas outsourcing—when possible, we should try to work with those around us rather than cutting costs and destroying communities

While big tech and large corporate solutions can offer deliver slick, "one size fits all" kinds of projects, this is often done in the manner of the Bed of Procrustes—adjusting the customer to the shape of the project, rather than the other way around! Its no wonder the kids' heads are shrinking.

We aim to do just the opposite—bringing people back to the front and leverage existing tech products to design robust, easily maintainable systems that make nerds elsewhere manage the small, costly details and research and development of platforms we don't control.

About Us

Postnerd is a "lean startup" aiming at providing low cost, low maintenance technology solutions to people who share similar concerns with us about the societal effects of the spread of digital technologies. We seek to empower our customers by showing them how they can use technologies to work for them without expensive contracts, privacy invasive marketing schemes, or over-paying nerds to try to describe people with numbers in databases.

Our name "postnerd" means "after (being a) nerd"—by training, many tech professionals are taught to be highly specialized, interchangeable parts. Our philosophy is the opposite—we recognize the uniqueness of each individual and how custom solutions using common and easy to use tools are often the most cost-effective solutions for actually getting things done.

We created postnerd to do something about some disturbing trends that really got going in the twenty-first century. Specifically, the arrival of the smartphone has ushered in an un-precedented era of centralized control and surveillance with the decisions of very small, un-elected groups of people exercising massive influence (and thus control through subtle suggestion) of millions of people.

We are not "anti-technology". As can be seen below, we make use of social media, sites like those in the links below. To the contrary, we strive to extract maximum value from those services freely offered to us. We aren't zealots for anonymity or privacy—ID verification has its place in society. On the other hand, we don't believe you should ask for and retain information longer than is needed to conduct honest business.

We look forward to getting in touch with you!
